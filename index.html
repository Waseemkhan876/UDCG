Our Services
Estimation

In a civil consulting business, estimation entails precisely projecting the resources, time, and cost needed for building projects. It entails assessing labour requirements, material amounts, and project scope in addition to making sure that the budget is followed and lowering risks. Project efficiency, on-time completion, and conformity to customer expectations are all guaranteed by accurate estimating.

RCC design

In a civil consulting business, estimating RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) entails figuring out the amounts of materials, labour expenses, and project schedules needed for RCC construction. This entails evaluating formwork, concrete, reinforcing steel, and other relevant components to guarantee accuracy, cost effectiveness, and adherence to quality standards and design requirements.

Hydraulic design

In civil consulting, hydraulic design entails developing effective water management systems, guaranteeing appropriate drainage, flood control, and sustainable water distribution. In order to maximise water flow and lower hazards in urban infrastructure projects, it entails planning stormwater systems, sewage networks, and irrigation schemes while taking regulatory compliance and environmental effects into account.

Architectural planning

In a civil consulting business, architectural planning entails creating environments that are sustainable, aesthetically beautiful, and useful. Regulatory compliance, space optimisation, conceptual design, and site investigation are all included. The procedure guarantees the effective use of resources while incorporating client needs and environmental factors to produce creative, safe, and useful buildings

Valuation

A civil consulting firm's assets, projects, and general operations are all evaluated for financial worth as part of the valuation process. It involves assessing both intangible assets like intellectual property and market position as well as tangible assets like infrastructure and machinery. Strategic planning, mergers, and investment choices are all aided by accurate appraisal.

Road safety audit

A road safety audit is a methodical assessment carried out to find any safety hazards on public roads. In order to guarantee user safety, a civil consulting business must evaluate infrastructure, traffic patterns, and road design. Suggestions are offered to reduce hazards, improving general traffic safety and standard compliance.

Structural audit

A structural audit examines the parts, materials, and systems of a building or infrastructure to determine its safety and integrity. It points out any possible problems like wear, corrosion, or cracks and offers suggestions for fixes or improvements. This procedure guarantees stability over the long run and adherence to safety regulations.

Testing of concrete

To guarantee quality and longevity, our civil consulting organisation offers thorough concrete testing services. Our testing techniques, which include compressive, strength, and slump tests, assist evaluate the uniformity, workability, and load-bearing capability of the mix and guarantee that your projects adhere to safety and legal requirements.

Testing of bitumen

In order to guarantee bitumen quality and performance in building projects, our civil consulting organisation offers thorough testing. In order to make sure that the bitumen satisfies the necessary requirements for stability, weather resistance, and durability in road construction, we offer tests for viscosity, penetration, softening point, and ductility.

Testing of bricks

We thoroughly examine bricks at our civil consulting agency to guarantee their quality, strength, and endurance. Compressive strength, water absorption, and density evaluations are among the tests we do to make sure materials fulfil industry requirements and are appropriate for building projects, resulting in sturdy and safe constructions.

Testing of soil

Our civil consulting organisation offers soil testing as a vital service to guarantee sustainable and safe construction. To inform design choices, we evaluate soil characteristics including permeability, compaction, and load-bearing capability. Precise soil testing ensures your project's long-term stability and safety by preventing structural problems.

Laboratory services

To guarantee the quality and safety of building materials, our laboratory services offer thorough testing and analysis. To ensure the successful completion of your projects, we provide a broad range of services, such as material performance analysis, concrete testing, and soil testing, all carried out in compliance with industry standards.

Consultancy services

At the civil consulting organisation, we specialise in providing customised digital solutions as part of our website consulting services. In order to ensure a smooth integration with the civil engineering projects undertaken by your company, we provide professional guidance on website design, functionality, and user experience. Our objective is to increase client interaction and your internet visibility.
